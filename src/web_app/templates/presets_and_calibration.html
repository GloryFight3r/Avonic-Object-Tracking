To add a preset type the name you want it to have and after that click 'Set camera position', which will record the direction where the camera is currently pointing. After that click 'Set microphone direction' to record the direction of the next noise. Finally, click 'Add new preset'.
To edit a preset select the preset you want to change from the preset list and record new camera direction and/or microphone direction in the same manner.
To remove a preset, select it from the list and click 'Remove preset'.
